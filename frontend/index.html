What's Trending

Industry-Specific "Industry Cloud" Solutions

SAP's vertical-focused cloud offerings for sectors like manufacturing, healthcare, and retail are gaining traction, enabling clients to implement tailored, standards-based solutions faster.

Real-Time Analytics with SAP Analytics Cloud & Datasphere

The live integration between SAC and Datasphere supports seamless data models, real-time planning, and predictive analytics — eliminating batch replication and enhancing insights.

Embedded AI Across SAP Applications

SAP is embedding AI — including generative AI tools like Joule Everywhere — across its suite to power intelligent workflows, natural-language assistants, and process automation in finance, procurement, HR, and more.

Cloud Migration & SAP S/4HANA Push

With ECC mainstream support ending in 2027, businesses are rapidly migrating to SAP S/4HANA — especially via RISE with SAP or GROW options — to meet compliance, scalability, and agility needs.
